Left 4 Dead 2 Hub - Site Plan

Site Name

Proposed Name: Left 4 Dead 2 Hub

Reason: This name is simple, clear, and directly informs users that the site is about the game Left 4 Dead 2. It’s easy to remember and highlights the website as a central information source.

Site Purpose

This website will provide detailed information about the game Left 4 Dead 2, including its mechanics, lore, characters, and infected types. It aims to introduce new players to the game and deepen fans' appreciation of its elements.

New features will include interactive elements such as a weather API integration for in-game weather tracking, mod-related content for players looking to expand their experience, and improved UI/UX design to enhance accessibility and engagement.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: "Who are the characters in Left 4 Dead 2, and what makes each of them unique?"

Scenario 2: "Who are the special infected and common infected, and what are their abilities?"

Scenario 3: "How can I check the in-game weather effects and how do they impact gameplay?"

Scenario 4: "Where can I find information about Left 4 Dead 2 mods and how to install them?"

Color Schema

Primary Color: Bright Green (#2fd345) - Used for navigation bars, headings, and accents.

Secondary Color: Light Gray (#D9D9D9) - Used for backgrounds and body text.

Accent Color: Red (#d32f2f) - For buttons, links, or important highlights.

Typography

Heading Font: Bebas Neue - Used for titles and headings.

Body Font: Roboto - Used for paragraphs and general text.
